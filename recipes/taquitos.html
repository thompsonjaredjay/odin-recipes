Air Fryer Chicken Taquitos

Description

Along with chicken bakes I would also eat El Monterey chicken taquitos from Costco. I know, I am surprised I did not get fat. This recipe is not a copycat but they are fast, and easy to make. You can serve with sour cream, Mexican tomato sauce, and guacamole.

This recipe has a 10 minute prep time and a 20 minute cook time for a total time of 30 minutes. It yields 6 taquitos with a serving size of 1 taquito.
