Air Fryer Chicken Taquitos

Description

Along with chicken bakes I would also eat El Monterey chicken taquitos from Costco. I know, I am surprised I did not get fat. This recipe is not a copycat but they are fast, and easy to make. You can serve with sour cream, Mexican tomato sauce, and guacamole.

This recipe has a 10 minute prep time and a 20 minute cook time for a total time of 30 minutes. It yields 6 taquitos with a serving size of 1 taquito.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons diced onion
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green chiles
  • 2 tablespoons Mexican-style got tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded rotisserie chicken
  • 1/2 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • 2 tablespoons Neufchâtel cheese
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • 6 corn tortillas
  • avocado oil cooking spray

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add onion; cook until soft and translucent, 3 to 5 minutes
  2. Add garlic; cook until fragrant, about 1 minute
  3. Add green chiles and Mexican tomato sauce; stir to combine
  4. Add shredded chicken, Mexican cheese blend, and Neufchâtel cheese
  5. Cook and stir until cheeses have melted and mixture is warmed through, about 3 minutes
  6. Season with salt and pepper
  7. Heat tortillas in a skillet or directly on the grates of a gas stove until soft and pliable
  8. Place 3 tablespoons of chicken mixture down the center of each tortilla. Fold over and roll into taquitos
  9. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
  10. Place taquitos in a single layer in the air fryer basket, making sure they are not touching, and mist with avocado oil
  11. Cook until golden brown, 6 to 9 minutes. Turn taquitos over, mist with avocado oil, and air fry until crispy, 3 to 5 minutes
  12. Serve and enjoy!
